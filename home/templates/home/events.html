The Royal Canal Run

The Brendan Doyle Running Club's annual race event started in 2015 with the goal of bringing together running enthusiasts of all levels to participate in a challenging yet enjoyable race experience. Held on the banks of the Royal Canal, Longford, the race has grown in popularity over the years, attracting participants from across the region and beyond. With a range of distances including 10K, Half Marathon, Marathon, and the 53K Ultramarathon, there is a challenge suitable for everyone, and the route is also suitable for wheelchair participants. The event is not only a test of speed and endurance but also a celebration of community spirit and the joy of running. Whether you're a seasoned runner or new to the sport, the Brendan Doyle Running Club's race offers a fantastic opportunity to push your limits and achieve your personal best.
