Welcome to Brendan Doyle Running Club

Running For All

Located in the heart of Co. Longford, Ireland, the Brendan Doyle Running Club is dedicated to runners of all ages and fitness levels. Whether you are an experienced marathoner or just starting out, we offer a supportive and inclusive environment to help you achieve your running goals.

Join us for regular training sessions, fun runs, and community events. Our club is more than just running; it's about building a community, fostering friendships, and promoting a healthy lifestyle.

Experience the joy of running with Brendan Doyle Running Club. Let's make every step count!
