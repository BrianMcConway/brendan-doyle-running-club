About Us

Welcome to the Brendan Doyle Running Club! Located in the beautiful Co. Longford, Ireland, our club is a vibrant community of running enthusiasts of all ages and fitness levels. Whether you are a seasoned runner or just beginning your running journey, you will find a place here.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to promote the joy of running and foster a supportive, inclusive environment for all runners. We believe that running is for everyone, and our slogan, "Running For All," reflects our commitment to inclusivity and community spirit.

What We Offer

  • Regular group training sessions to help you stay motivated and improve your fitness.
  • Fun runs and social events to build camaraderie and make running enjoyable.
  • Opportunities to participate in local, national, and international running events.
  • Support and advice from experienced runners and coaches.

Our Community

At Brendan Doyle Running Club, we pride ourselves on our welcoming and friendly atmosphere. Our members range from beginners to experienced marathoners, and we all share a passion for running and a commitment to supporting one another. Join us and become part of a community that inspires, motivates, and celebrates every runner’s achievements.

Join Us

If you’re interested in joining the Brendan Doyle Running Club, we’d love to hear from you! Please contact us or come along to one of our training sessions to see what we’re all about. Together, we can make running a fun and fulfilling part of your life.
